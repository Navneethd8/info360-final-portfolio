The Design Solution

Content in progress

An AI-powered platform that matches UW students into compatible, class-wise study groups based on schedule, personality, learning style, and interests.

Core concept: Proactively pair students before the awkward "does anyone want to study?" moment — reducing social risk with profile previews, transparent match reasoning, and a structured first session.
